## How to Use New Features

### Adding Video to Article
1. Go to Admin → Articles → Create/Edit Article
2. Scroll to "Video URL (Optional)" field
3. Enter either:
   - YouTube URL: `https://youtube.com/watch?v=VIDEO_ID`
   - Direct video: `https://example.com/video.mp4`
4. Video will appear in article with professional player

### Adding PDF to Article
1. Go to Admin → Articles → Create/Edit Article
2. Scroll to "PDF Document URL (Optional)" field
3. Enter PDF URL: `https://example.com/document.pdf`
4. PDF will be embedded with download button
